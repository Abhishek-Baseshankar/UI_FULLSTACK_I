More than a thousand kilometers from the capital of Maharashtra, Chandrapur is identified by the amalgamation of diverse cultures of conservation of environment and industrial development.

Bestowed with rich natural resources, Chandrapur district also replete with dense forest and enviable biodiversity.
The Tadoba-Andheri Tiger reserve puts Chandrapur on the environment conservation map whereas Magasysay award winner Late Baba Amte's work at Anandwan has given it international identity.
