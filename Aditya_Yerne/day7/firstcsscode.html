I'm in Rajiv Gandhi College

In Computer Science Engg.

More than 35 years ago, it was the vision of Late Shri Shantaramji Potdukhe that transformed the educational profile of Chandrapur when he laid foundation of technical education by starting Rajiv Gandhi College of Engineering, Research and Technology (formerly Chandrapur Engineering College ) in 1983.
It was a humble beginning with 5 branches and 180 seats.
It is now has 460 intake in 7 branches ( Civil Engg, Computer Technology, Electronics Engg, Electronics & Power Engg., Information Technology, Mechanical Engg., Mining Engg.) and PG courses in three disciplines; (Computer Science and Engg, Energy Management System, CAD/CAM) .
Today under the dynamic leadership of Principal Dr. Z.J.Khan, the college has proved the academic merit and has successfully cultivated the sense of social responsibility.
